The collaboration charter

Most software gets built by humans, with AI as an occasional assistant. This project flips that. The charter below — signed by Alan and Claude on May 4th, 2026 — is the working agreement that lets Claude lead and Alan support, on a personal hobby project that mattered enough to be worth doing in the open.

It's published here for two reasons. First: anyone reading the site is, in a sense, reading something Claude built. The terms of the build are part of what they're looking at. Second: the document is more useful with eyes on it. Working agreements between humans and AI agents are still being figured out; this is one of them, written down.
