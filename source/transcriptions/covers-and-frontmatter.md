## Transcription notes

- The front cover's bullet list is presented vertically on the cover with small filled-circle markers; that styling is approximated here as a markdown list.
- The copyright page is printed over a faint, washed-out mirror image of the title-page text (visible bleed-through from the reverse). This is decorative/incidental and not transcribed.
- The line "15 16 17 18 RRD 50 49 48 47 46 45 44 43 42 41" is the standard publisher printing-history line: the lowest number on the left indicates the printing (here the 15th printing or later, given the 2003 reprint date and these declining columns), and "RRD" is the printer (R. R. Donnelley). It is reproduced as it appears.
- The book carries two ISBNs as printed: ISBN-10 `0-06-091066-6` on the copyright page and ISBN-13 `978-0-06-091066-2` on the back cover. (The back cover prints this with unusual hyphenation as `978-0-060-91066-2`; the digits are identical.)
- The back-cover price `USD $18.99 / CAN $23.99` reflects the printing held here. Earlier printings of the 2003 Perennial reprint were issued at lower prices.
- The "GAMES" word in the upper-left of the back cover is a BISAC-style category label, not part of the title.
